📖 About This App

MRX Quize is not just a quiz app — it's a complete learning platform designed to make grammar practice simple, interactive, and engaging. Instead of traditional methods, it focuses on a smooth user experience, modern interface, and smart learning approach.

Along with grammar practice, the app offers subject-wise quizzes covering multiple topics up to class 12. Users can choose quizzes based on their preference, including both Hindi and English modes, making it flexible and suitable for different learning needs.

With continuous updates, improvements, and new ideas, MRX Quize is evolving into a powerful educational tool where users not only learn but also enjoy the complete experience.
